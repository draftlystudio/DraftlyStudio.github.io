About Us

Mechanical CAD Specialists Supporting Manufacturing & Fabrication

Draftly Engineering provides professional AutoCAD drafting and SolidWorks modeling services focused on real production needs. We convert hand sketches, PDF drawings, and conceptual ideas into accurate engineering models, assemblies, and fabrication documentation. Our workflow emphasizes dimensional accuracy, tolerance awareness, manufacturability, and compatibility with CNC machining, laser cutting, sheet metal fabrication, and industrial production pipelines.
